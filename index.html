A Good Start

Your daily journaling helper

How does it work?

Each set of prompts offered below consist of 2 to 5 questions. Choose what you feel like. Enter text in the boxes. Send a summary to your email, or to your notes, or download in the format you want.

Find out more about the different prompts

Morning Pages: A freewriting exercise to clear your mind at the start of the day.

Future Self: Prompts to help you envision the person you want to become.

Morning Productivity: Questions designed to kickstart your day with focus and purpose.

Gratitude: Simple reflections to remind you of the things you're grateful for.

Manifestation: Focuses on setting intentions and visualizing your goals.
